Welcome to tobilehman.com. (Yes the terminal is real)

This site has my blog posts, the projects I've worked on, and a section about me. I write about books I've read, technologies I've worked with, programs I've written, and historical developments that I find interesting.

I believe every problem can be solved. I also acknowledge that every solution creates it's own problems. Overall, I believe that the rate of solution-production is greater than the rate of problem-production, which means that human life is getting better over time. Further, I believe it's our moral responsibility to understand the root causes of that progress, and to do whatever we can to continue and even accelerate that progress.

I chose to work on software enabling remote control of robots. It is a complement to autonomy software, whose success means no human intervention. We are in a transitional period in history where AI still lags behind general human intelligence, and the COVID-19 pandemic has forced a lot of people into remote work. The tools for doing office-style remote work are mature. Remote manual labor is still in its infancy. There is tremendous room for progress in this area.

Storrs Hall Started (2021-12-06), we are planning on meeting up to talk about it sometime before new years. If you are interested, go to the Google doc and we can take notes and plan there.
