Welcome to tobilehman.com (the terminal is real). This site has my blog posts, the projects I've worked on, and a section about me.

I write about time, math, programming, and books I've read.

I believe every problem can be solved. I also acknowledge that every solution creates it's own problems. But I think the rate of solution-production is greater than the rate of problem-production, which means that human life is getting better over time. Further, I believe it's our moral responsibility to understand the root causes of that progress, and to do whatever we can to continue and even accelerate that progress.
